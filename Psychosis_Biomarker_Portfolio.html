System Design

Key Design Decisions

🏗️
Role-Based Entry Pathway
Coordinators manage enrollment decisions and status flags. Medical students and trainees follow a scoped chart-abstraction pathway, reducing error risk and maintaining data integrity across a multi-user team.
🔄
Longitudinal Open Follow-Up
Cases remain open for reassessment when new information becomes available — delayed antibody results, updated imaging, or evolving clinical presentation can all trigger re-review without data loss.
⚙️
Automated Clinical Reasoning
@CALCTEXT annotations automatically display Graus Criteria status, APE-2 outcomes, MoCA cognitive categorization, and MSE insight interpretation — reducing manual scoring burden for every entry.
📊
Structured Checklists
Critical chart review items must be documented before enrollment status can be finalized, ensuring completeness and reproducibility across all study personnel regardless of experience level.
🧩
Integrated Scoring Workflows
BPRS and APE-2 scoring are embedded directly in the workflow rather than calculated externally — the instrument guides the scorer and automatically computes the result at the point of data entry.
🔒
Reproducible Framework
Full project export (XML + data dictionary) enables recreation of the complete system in any REDCap instance. De-identified for portfolio sharing while preserving all logic, field structure, and workflows.
